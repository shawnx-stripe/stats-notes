# heteroskedasticity

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Heteroskedasticity means the conditional variance of an error term changes with covariates or fitted values.

## When it matters

OLS coefficients can remain unbiased under exogeneity, but conventional homoskedastic standard errors are wrong. Use robust, cluster-robust, or model-based variance methods aligned to the design.

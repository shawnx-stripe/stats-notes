# policy endogeneity

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Policy endogeneity occurs when policy adoption, timing, or intensity responds to expected outcomes or shocks.

## When it matters

It threatens DiD, event-study, and quasi-experimental designs because treated units may already be on different trajectories. Use institutional evidence, pre-trends, placebo policies, and alternative comparison groups.

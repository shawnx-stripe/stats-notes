# consistency

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Consistency links observed outcomes to potential outcomes: a unit observed under treatment value d has outcome Y(d).

## When it matters

It requires well-defined treatment versions and outcome measurement. Violations arise with hidden treatment versions, interference, or ambiguous exposure definitions.

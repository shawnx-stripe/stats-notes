# uplift metrics

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Uplift metrics evaluate how well a model ranks units by incremental treatment effect.

## When it matters

Qini curves, AUUC, and policy value focus on targeting gains rather than outcome prediction accuracy. Use held-out or cross-fit scores and known treatment propensities for unbiased evaluation.

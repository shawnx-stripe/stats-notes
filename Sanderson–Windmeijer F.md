# Sanderson–Windmeijer F

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The Sanderson-Windmeijer F statistic diagnoses weak identification for each endogenous regressor in models with multiple endogenous variables.

## When it matters

A single first-stage F can hide that one endogenous regressor is weakly identified conditional on the others. Report conditional diagnostics alongside first stages and weak-IV robust inference.

# donut RDD

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Donut RDD re-estimates a regression discontinuity design after excluding observations very close to the cutoff.

## When it matters

It is a robustness check for manipulation, heaping, sorting, or measurement error exactly at the threshold. The excluded window should be justified and varied in sensitivity analysis.

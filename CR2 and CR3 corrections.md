# CR2 and CR3 corrections

> [!summary] Quick definition
> CR2 and CR3 are cluster-robust variance corrections designed to improve inference with few or unbalanced clusters.

## When it matters

They adjust cluster leverage and usually pair with Satterthwaite-style degrees of freedom. They are a practical alternative or complement to the wild cluster bootstrap when cluster counts are limited.

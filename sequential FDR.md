# sequential FDR

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Sequential FDR controls false discoveries across a stream of tests that arrive over time.

## When it matters

It is relevant for experimentation platforms and monitoring systems that repeatedly launch tests. Procedures such as alpha investing allocate error budgets adaptively across the stream.

# curse of dimensionality

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The curse of dimensionality is the rapid growth in data requirements as feature dimension increases.

## When it matters

Nonparametric smoothing, matching, and nearest-neighbor methods can become unstable in high dimensions because local neighborhoods are sparse. Dimension reduction or structured models are often needed.

# policy invariance

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Policy invariance asks whether behavioral relationships estimated under one policy regime remain stable under a new regime.

## When it matters

Reduced-form effects may not transport when incentives, equilibrium behavior, or composition change. Structural arguments, mechanism evidence, and sensitivity analysis are needed for large policy changes.

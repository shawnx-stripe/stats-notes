# demeaning

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Demeaning subtracts a mean from a variable, often by unit, time, or group.

## When it matters

In panel models, demeaning removes fixed effects; in interaction models, centering can make main effects easier to interpret. The mean used determines the estimand.

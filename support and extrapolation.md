# support and extrapolation

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Support and extrapolation describe whether comparisons are made where the data actually contain comparable units.

## When it matters

Poor support makes estimates model-dependent and can invalidate transport. Diagnose overlap, trim unsupported regions, and state the population for which the estimate is credible.

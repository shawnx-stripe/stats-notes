# covariate balance test

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A covariate balance test checks whether pre-treatment covariates are similar across treatment or assignment contrasts.

## When it matters

In experiments it diagnoses randomization integrity; in RD it checks smoothness at the cutoff; in observational designs it assesses adjustment quality. Balance is supportive evidence, not proof of identification.

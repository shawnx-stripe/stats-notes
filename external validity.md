# external validity

> [!summary] Quick definition
> External validity is the credibility of applying study findings beyond the analyzed sample, setting, or treatment version.

## When it matters

A design can be internally valid but still local to compliers, a cutoff, a donor pool, or a rollout context. Discuss target populations, mechanisms, implementation differences, and support.

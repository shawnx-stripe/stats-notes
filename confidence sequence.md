# confidence sequence

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A confidence sequence is a sequence of intervals that covers a parameter uniformly over time with a stated probability.

## When it matters

Confidence sequences support continuous monitoring without invalidating coverage. They are usually wider than fixed-time intervals but shrink as information accumulates.

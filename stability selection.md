# stability selection

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Stability selection repeatedly fits a variable-selection method on subsamples and keeps variables selected frequently.

## When it matters

It helps assess whether selected features are robust to sampling variation, especially with lasso-like methods. Selection frequency is descriptive unless paired with formal error-control assumptions.

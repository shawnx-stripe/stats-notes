# 2SRI

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Two-stage residual inclusion estimates a first-stage treatment model and includes its residual in a nonlinear second-stage outcome model.

## When it matters

2SRI is a control-function approach for endogenous treatments in nonlinear models such as logit, probit, or count outcomes. Interpretation depends on the structural model and valid instruments.

# nonstationary bandits

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Nonstationary bandits allow arm rewards to change over time.

## When it matters

They are relevant when seasonality, market conditions, or product changes alter treatment performance. Algorithms need recency weighting, change detection, or explicit time-varying models.

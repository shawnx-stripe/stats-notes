# model extrapolation

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Model extrapolation uses a fitted model to predict outside the support well represented in the data.

## When it matters

Extrapolation can dominate causal estimates when treatment and control groups have poor overlap. Diagnose support, trim where needed, and avoid relying on functional form alone.

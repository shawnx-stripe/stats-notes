# Conley et al. plausibly exogenous

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Conley et al. plausibly exogenous methods relax exact IV exclusion by allowing bounded direct effects of instruments on outcomes.

## When it matters

Use this as sensitivity analysis when an instrument is credible but small exclusion violations are plausible. Specify a defensible range for the direct effect and report how IV conclusions change across that range.

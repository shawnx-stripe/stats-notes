# spatial buffers

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Spatial buffers exclude or separate units within a chosen distance of treated areas, boundaries, or likely spillover zones.

## When it matters

They help diagnose or reduce contamination but can alter the estimand. Vary buffer distances and report how many units are excluded.

# Cragg–Donald statistic

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The Cragg-Donald statistic is a homoskedastic weak-instrument diagnostic for IV models with multiple instruments.

## When it matters

It is the reference statistic behind Stock-Yogo critical values under iid homoskedastic errors. With heteroskedasticity or clustering, prefer robust diagnostics such as Kleibergen-Paap or Montiel Olea-Pflueger effective F.

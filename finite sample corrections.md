# finite sample corrections

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Finite sample corrections adjust variance estimates or reference distributions when asymptotic approximations are poor.

## When it matters

They are important with few clusters, high leverage, small samples, or many parameters relative to observations. Corrections should match the estimator and design rather than be applied mechanically.

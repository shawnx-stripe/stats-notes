# standard error

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A standard error is the estimated standard deviation of an estimator's sampling distribution.

## When it matters

Standard errors feed t-statistics, confidence intervals, and power calculations. They must reflect clustering, serial correlation, heteroskedasticity, weighting, or design features when those are present.

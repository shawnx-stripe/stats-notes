# Fisher information

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Fisher information measures the curvature of the log-likelihood and the amount of information a sample carries about a parameter.

## When it matters

It underlies asymptotic standard errors for maximum likelihood estimators and efficiency bounds. Observed and expected information can differ in finite samples or misspecified models.

# spatial autocorrelation

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Spatial autocorrelation means observations closer in space tend to have more similar outcomes or errors.

## When it matters

It can invalidate iid or cluster-only standard errors and can signal unmodeled spillovers. Diagnose with maps, Moran's I, and distance-based residual checks.

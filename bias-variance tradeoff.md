# bias-variance tradeoff

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The bias-variance tradeoff describes how estimator error decomposes into systematic approximation error and sampling variability.

## When it matters

Flexible models often lower bias but raise variance, while regularization does the reverse. In causal work, low prediction error does not remove identification bias.

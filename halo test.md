# halo test

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A halo test checks whether units near treated areas show effects consistent with spillovers.

## When it matters

It is useful in geo experiments and marketplace interventions where treatment can leak to neighboring regions or users. Distance bands and pre-specified exposure definitions reduce fishing.

# anytime p-values

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Anytime p-values remain valid under optional stopping and continuous monitoring.

## When it matters

They are useful when analysts cannot pre-specify a small number of interim looks. They are typically built from test martingales or e-values and should be chosen before looking at outcomes.

# mixture SPRT

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A mixture SPRT integrates likelihood ratios over an alternative distribution to create an always-valid sequential test.

## When it matters

It is used for continuous monitoring in online experiments when a fixed alternative is too narrow. The mixture distribution encodes the effect sizes the test is optimized to detect.

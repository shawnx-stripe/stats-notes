# spillover estimands

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Spillover estimands define causal contrasts when outcomes depend on own treatment and others' treatment.

## When it matters

Common contrasts include direct effects holding exposure fixed, indirect effects changing exposure among untreated units, total effects, and overall policy effects. The exposure mapping must be explicit.

# weight trimming and caps

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Weight trimming or capping limits extreme inverse-probability or balancing weights.

## When it matters

It reduces variance from poor overlap but changes the estimand toward the retained or capped population. Report thresholds, effective sample size, and sensitivity to alternatives.

# targeting with constraints

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Targeting with constraints chooses treatment rules subject to budgets, capacity, fairness, or guardrail requirements.

## When it matters

The optimal unconstrained CATE threshold may violate operational or ethical constraints. Define the constraint, estimate policy value under it, and validate online when possible.

# GATES

> [!summary] Quick definition
> GATES groups units by predicted treatment effects and estimates average treatment effects within those groups.

## When it matters

It is a practical way to validate whether a CATE model ranks heterogeneity meaningfully. Use honest sample splitting so groups are formed out-of-sample.

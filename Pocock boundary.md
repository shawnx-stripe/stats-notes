# Pocock boundary

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A Pocock boundary uses approximately constant stopping thresholds across interim looks.

## When it matters

It spends more alpha early than O'Brien-Fleming, making early stopping easier but final thresholds more stringent. Use when early decisions are valuable and planned looks are limited.

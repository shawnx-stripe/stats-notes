# MCAR

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Missing Completely At Random means the probability that data are missing is unrelated to observed or unobserved values.

## When it matters

Under MCAR, complete-case analysis is unbiased for population means and regression relationships, though it can lose precision. It is a strong assumption and is often checked indirectly by comparing observed covariates across missingness groups.

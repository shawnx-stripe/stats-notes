# variable importance

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Variable importance measures how much a feature contributes to a model's predictions or splits.

## When it matters

Importance is model- and metric-specific and is not a causal effect. Check sensitivity to correlated features, leakage, and whether the measure is permutation-, impurity-, or SHAP-based.

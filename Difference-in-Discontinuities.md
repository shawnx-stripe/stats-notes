# Difference-in-Discontinuities

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Difference-in-discontinuities compares RD jumps before and after a policy or across treated and comparison boundaries.

## When it matters

It combines RD-style local comparisons with differencing to remove stable discontinuities unrelated to treatment. The design needs both RD continuity near the cutoff and a credible differencing comparison.

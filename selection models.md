# selection models

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Selection models jointly model the outcome process and the probability that the outcome is observed.

## When it matters

They are used when missingness or sample inclusion may depend on unobserved outcomes. Identification typically needs distributional assumptions, exclusion restrictions, or sensitivity parameters.

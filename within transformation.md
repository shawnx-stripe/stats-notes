# within transformation

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The within transformation subtracts unit means to remove time-invariant fixed effects from panel regressions.

## When it matters

It is the algebra behind fixed-effects estimators. It identifies effects from within-unit changes and drops variables that do not vary within unit.

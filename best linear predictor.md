# best linear predictor

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A best linear predictor projects true or estimated treatment effects onto a set of covariates or scores.

## When it matters

In causal ML, BLP diagnostics test whether predicted CATEs are calibrated and whether treatment effects vary with a score. Interpret it as a projection, not proof of individual-level effects.

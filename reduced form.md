# reduced form

> [!summary] Quick definition
> The reduced form is the effect of an instrument or assignment on the outcome before scaling by treatment take-up.

## When it matters

In IV, the reduced form is the numerator of the Wald estimand. It is often policy-relevant on its own when assignment, eligibility, or encouragement is the actionable intervention.

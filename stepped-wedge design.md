# stepped-wedge design

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A stepped-wedge design rolls treatment out to clusters in randomized waves until all clusters receive treatment.

## When it matters

It is useful when universal rollout is planned but timing can be randomized. Analysis must adjust for time effects, clustering, and potential anticipation or spillovers.

# RD plot

> [!summary] Quick definition
> An RD plot bins the running variable and shows outcome means around the cutoff with fitted local trends.

## When it matters

It is a diagnostic and communication tool, not the estimator itself. Use it to inspect discontinuities, curvature, outliers, and support near the cutoff before reporting formal RD estimates.

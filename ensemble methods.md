# ensemble methods

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Ensemble methods combine multiple models to improve prediction stability or accuracy.

## When it matters

Bagging reduces variance, boosting reduces bias through sequential fitting, and random forests combine both subsampling and feature randomness. For causal use, separate prediction performance from identification.

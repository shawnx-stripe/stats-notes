# delayed outcomes

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Delayed outcomes are responses observed after a lag from treatment exposure or assignment.

## When it matters

They complicate interim monitoring, bandits, and triggered analyses because early data are censored or biased toward fast responders. Predefine attribution windows and delay adjustments.

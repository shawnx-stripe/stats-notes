# O’Brien–Fleming boundary

> [!summary] Quick definition
> An O'Brien-Fleming boundary uses very stringent early stopping thresholds and approaches conventional thresholds near the final look.

## When it matters

It is conservative early, making it attractive when premature stopping is costly. It is usually implemented through group-sequential software or alpha spending approximations.

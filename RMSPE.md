# RMSPE

> [!summary] Quick definition
> RMSPE is the square root of the average squared prediction error.

## When it matters

In synthetic control, pre-treatment RMSPE summarizes fit and is often used to filter or scale placebo comparisons. Low RMSPE supports fit but not identification by itself.

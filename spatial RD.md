# spatial RD

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Spatial RD identifies effects at geographic boundaries where treatment changes discontinuously across space.

## When it matters

The key assumption is continuity of potential outcomes at the border absent treatment. Diagnostics should check local balance, bandwidth sensitivity, spatial sorting, and spatial correlation in inference.

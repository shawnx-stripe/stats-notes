# robustness checks

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Robustness checks examine whether conclusions persist under defensible alternative specifications, samples, or assumptions.

## When it matters

They should target the design's main threats rather than enumerate arbitrary variants. Pre-specify key checks when possible and report failures transparently.

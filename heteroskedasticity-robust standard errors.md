# heteroskedasticity-robust standard errors

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Heteroskedasticity-robust standard errors estimate regression uncertainty without assuming constant error variance.

## When it matters

HC estimators are a default for cross-sectional regressions with possible variance misspecification. They do not handle serial, cluster, or spatial dependence by themselves.

# batched bandits

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Batched bandits update allocation policies only after groups of observations rather than after every unit.

## When it matters

Batching fits operational systems with delayed metrics or scheduled deployments. It trades slower learning for simpler infrastructure and cleaner logging.

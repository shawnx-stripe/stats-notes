# Love plot

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A Love plot displays standardized covariate differences before and after adjustment.

## When it matters

It is a compact balance diagnostic for matching, weighting, or stratification. Large remaining standardized differences indicate model or support problems that may require trimming or revised adjustment.

# sandwich estimator

> [!summary] Quick definition
> A sandwich estimator uses a bread-meat-bread form to estimate variance robustly under certain forms of misspecification.

## When it matters

It appears in M-estimation, GMM, robust standard errors, and clustered covariance estimators. The meat must reflect the dependence structure, such as iid, clustered, HAC, or spatial correlation.

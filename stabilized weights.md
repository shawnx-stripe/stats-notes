# stabilized weights

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Stabilized weights multiply inverse probability weights by a marginal or reduced-model numerator to reduce variance.

## When it matters

They are common in marginal structural models and longitudinal IPW. Stabilization helps precision but does not solve poor overlap or misspecified propensity models.

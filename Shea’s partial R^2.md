# Shea’s partial R^2

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Shea's partial R-squared measures instrument relevance after accounting for collinearity among instruments and endogenous regressors.

## When it matters

It is useful when instruments are numerous or correlated. Low Shea partial R-squared can reveal weak identification even when ordinary first-stage R-squared looks high.

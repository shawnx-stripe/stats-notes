# fairness in treatment assignment

> [!summary] Quick definition
> Fairness in treatment assignment concerns whether allocation rules create unjustified disparities in access, burden, or benefit.

## When it matters

It matters in targeting, bandits, and experiments that allocate scarce or beneficial interventions. Define fairness constraints explicitly and evaluate both outcomes and exposure shares across groups.
